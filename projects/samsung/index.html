Samsung Mobile / Samsung Electronics

One of the largest mobile phone and tablet manufacturers in the world, Samsung chose us to redesign and build a corporate intranet site to aid in the communication and collaboration between employees in North America. The project called for us to connect to multiple locations across several different systems that handled communications, human resources, production, distribution, travel and expenses.

We worked with Samsung to develop a solution utilizing a .net nuke CMS system that ties through ASP.net to the legacy systems, allowing employees to use a single login to access every system necessary. We then created an attractive web design and user-friendly interface. Today the intranet is the primary Samsung employee information site, containing everything from human resources documents and local news to weather reports and cafeteria menus.
