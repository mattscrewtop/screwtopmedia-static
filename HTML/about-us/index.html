The Process

Get more customers. Grow your revenue. Start with web resources that grab attention and bring them back. Let us help you make that happen! That’s why we developed a detailed five-phase process that resonates in everything we do. It is the process we run our own business on, and has been proven to not only serve our diverse client base but also exceed their every expectation.

Pre-Discovery

To begin, we’ll learn about your goals and aspirations to develop a thorough understanding on how to best serve your company and your customers. We’ll work with you to form a high-level overview of your unique situation and construct a plan that will help us to deliver the best solution possible for your business.

Discovery

Once the plan is finalized and all details are ironed out, we’ll develop a custom architecture and project plan to make absolutely certain you are getting exactly what you need, perfectly tailored to your company’s digital goals.

Design

Our talented designers will work to understand every detail of your brand, voice and mission and how they combine to communicate to your customers. Using quality web design concepts, best practices and the right technology needed to flawlessly connect interact with your customers the instant they land on your site.

Development

This is where it becomes real. The functionality of the site is built as we develop the backbone of your company’s new digital identity. Before deployment, our web development professionals will tirelessly test the site to identify and eliminate any flaws to ensure complete functionality.

Deployment

We build it, you own it; but we help monitor your new site and can also train you to make sure you are fully prepared to maintain it. As your company grows, we’ll continue to help you develop your digital needs. And, our worry-free guarantee means we will also be available to you if a sudden need develops.

Our History

Every business has a beginning that usually includes some unique and interesting stories. Screwtop Media, LLC, established operations at the beginning of 2010. Without going into too much detail, the name of the company was derived from an evening spent at a local sports bar enjoying several frosty beverages … and we suspect that probably explains enough … so we will just leave the story behind the name of the business right there.

Screwtop Media was established by a small group of marketing and technology professionals that each offered a different area of expertise in the marketing and technology industries. They believed by coming together and working as a team, they could provide industry services more effectively and efficiently in comparison to the shops and agencies that were servicing the market at that time. Looking back at the challenges we have faced, along with our success and occasional failure, one can quickly see that our company was built on enthusiasm and determination … but most of all, integrity and commitment act as the foundation the company rests upon.

Brady Tinker, the President of Screwtop Media, was working as a local radio and TV sports broadcaster who covered the local professional sports scene. Joining Mr. Tinker in the formation of this partnership was Todd Brown, the CEO of Screwtop Media, and Matt Ferrell, the Chief Technology Officer. After deciding to establish the business partnership, the three of them began working to secure the remaining additional resources that would be necessary in order to effectively provide the types of interactive marketing technology solutions the business would be offering.  Upon further discussion and planning, it became clear that it was time to take advantage of and embrace the business opportunities they were facing. Matt, Brady and Todd worked together to assemble the rest of their corporate team, which included engineers, programmers, project managers, graphic designers, and program analysts. Once they finished assembling their staff … Screwtop Media was born.

Our Leadership

Todd Brown

(CEO & Managing Partner)

Todd Brown is a third-generation veteran of the US Army and graduate of Texas Tech University. After receiving his bachelor’s degree, Todd and his wife moved to the Dallas/Fort Worth metroplex to pursue his professional career. In 1996, Todd began working for a local Dallas interactive marketing company that introduced him to the world of digital technology. After many years working for various local and global interactive marketing agencies, he and his colleagues made the decision to start their own. Then in 2010, Todd along with Brady Tinker and Matt Ferrell, established Screwtop Media, LLC.

Brady Tinker

(President)

Brady is a graduate of Kansas State University, where he played Tennis and Golf and resides in the Dallas area along with his wife and five children. Brady has been a television and radio executive since 1996 with experience in all aspects of production, including on-air talent, editing, program syndication and sponsorship sales.
